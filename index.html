Beyonce and The Number Four

The number four has extreme sentimental value to Beyonce, so much so that she named her fourth studio album '4'!

Here are 4 reasons why Beyonce loves the number 4:

  1. Beyonce's birthday is on 4 September
  2. Her husband Jay-Z's birthday is on 4 December 4
  3. Beyonce's mum's birthday is on 4 January
  4. Beyonce and Jay'Z's wedding anniversary is on 4 April
In our interview with Beyonce, she gave us some insight into why the number 4 is so important to her: On 4th April 2008 — eight divided by two is four — Jay Z put a ring on it. So there you have it from the Queen's mouth!
