The Number Four

The number four has extremely sentimental value to Beyonce, so much so that she named her fourth studio album '4'. Here are 4 reasons why Beyonce loves the number 4:

  1. Beyonce's birthday is on 4 September
  2. Her husband Jay-Z's birthday is on 4 December 4
  3. Beyonce's mum's birthday is on 4 January
  4. Beyonce and Jay'Z's wedding anniversary is on 4 April
In her own words, Beyonce explained why she loves the number 4 On 4th April 2008 — eight divided by two is four — Jay Z put a ring on it
